BELIEFS


Worship of the Pale King

The bugs of Hallownest believed that the Pale King created the entire world and everything in it. Although he was an elusive figure who was rarely seen, the citizens revered him as a god. They worshipped him through the King's Idols, icons which depict the King, with each of them being tailored to its owner. Various statues and shrines to the Pale King can be found throughout Hallownest, such as the fountain in Ancient Basin and a shrine in Crystal Peak.

Worship of the Radiance

Before the arrival of the Pale King, the Radiance was worshipped by her descendants, the Moth Tribe. A statue was built on Hallownest's Crown in her image. After being nearly forgotten and causing the Infection, the Radiance is now worshipped by the Moss Prophet who preaches about her to several Mossy Vagabonds. Numerous other bugs became part of the union that the Radiance offers, whether willingly or not.

Followers of Unn

The Mosskin worshipped Unn, who made them from her dream. The Lake of Unn used to be her home, and the building next to it functioned as a place of worship. The Mosskin guard Greenpath, believing that someday Unn will call them back to the dream they were born from.
